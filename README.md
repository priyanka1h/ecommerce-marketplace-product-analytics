# E-Commerce Marketplace Product & Analytics Case Study

## Project Overview

This project analyzes marketplace customer behavior, retention, revenue performance, delivery operations, and customer experience signals to identify factors impacting business growth and long-term customer retention.

## Tools Used

* SQL (MySQL Workbench)
* Power BI
* Excel
* Notion

## Business Problem

The marketplace is experiencing:

* Low repeat purchase behavior
* Customer churn
* Delivery inefficiencies
* Revenue concentration among a limited customer base

The objective of this project is to identify key business challenges and recommend product and business improvements using data-driven insights.

## Analysis Performed

* Revenue Analysis
* Customer Analysis
* Retention & Churn Analysis
* Funnel Analysis
* Delivery & Operational Analysis
* Payment Analysis
* Product Category Analysis

## Key Business Insights

* A significant proportion of customers make only a single purchase, indicating retention challenges.
* Revenue is concentrated among a small segment of customers and regions.
* Delivery delays and operational inefficiencies may negatively impact customer experience.
* Order cancellations contribute to revenue loss and reduced conversion efficiency.

## Product Recommendations

1. Loyalty Program
2. Personalized Product Recommendations
3. Delivery ETA Improvements
4. Customer Re-engagement Campaigns

## Dashboard

A 5-page Power BI dashboard was developed to visualize:

* Revenue Performance
* Customer Behavior
* Retention & Churn
* Delivery Operations
* Product & Seller Performance
